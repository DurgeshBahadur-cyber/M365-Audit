Restricting guest user access to their own objects prevents guests from enumerating directory users, groups, and other resources. Guests should be able to collaborate on shared resources but not act as directory readers for the entire tenant.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Entra admin center > External Identities > External collaboration settings
