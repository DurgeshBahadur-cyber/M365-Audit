The Global Administrator role added as a local admin on all Entra-joined devices means that a compromised GA account has local administrator rights across every managed Windows device — far exceeding the access needed for cloud administration.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Entra admin center > Devices > Device settings > Global administrator is added as local administrator on the device during Azure AD Join > No
