Chat resource-specific consent allows apps to access Teams chat content for specific chats or channels without requiring full organizational admin consent. Malicious apps distributed via this mechanism can access all messages in the chats they are granted consent for.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Teams admin center > Teams apps > Permission policies
