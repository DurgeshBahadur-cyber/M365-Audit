IP allow lists in the connection filter bypass all spam and malware filtering for connections from listed IPs. Shared hosting IPs, CDN exit nodes, and business partner ranges can be abused to relay malicious email that completely bypasses filtering.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Exchange admin center > Protection > Connection filter > Edit the policy and remove all IP allow list entries
