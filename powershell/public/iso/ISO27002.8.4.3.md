The meeting lobby provides a gate where organizers control when external attendees can join. Allowing anyone to bypass the lobby gives external participants immediate access to the meeting without organizer approval.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Teams admin center > Meetings > Meeting policies > Global > Who can bypass the lobby > People in my org
