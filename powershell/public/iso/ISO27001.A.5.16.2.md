Permanent eligible assignments allow role activation at any time without expiry. Time-bound eligible assignments limit the window during which a compromised account can activate a privileged role — after expiry, the assignment must be renewed through a governed process.

#### Remediation action:

Set a time-bound expiry on eligible assignments for Tier-0 roles. . Remove existing permanent eligible assignments and reissue as time-bound

Configure via: Entra admin center > Identity Governance > PIM > Microsoft Entra roles > select role > Settings > Edit > Assignment tab > Expire eligible assignments after (e.g., 12 months)
