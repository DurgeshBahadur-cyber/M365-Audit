Teams messages and meeting recordings constitute business records subject to the same retention requirements as email. Without a retention policy, Teams data can be permanently deleted, destroying evidence of conversations, decisions, and security-relevant activity.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Microsoft Purview > Data lifecycle management > Retention policies > Create or edit a retention policy to include Teams channel messages and chats
