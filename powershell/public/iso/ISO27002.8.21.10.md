Conditional Access policies that target directory roles by name only protect role members at policy evaluation time. Active role assignments added after policy creation are not covered until the policy explicitly includes them. Tier-0 roles without CA coverage are high-privilege accounts with no authentication requirements enforced by CA.

#### Remediation action:

Consider creating CA policies that specifically target privileged directory roles with stricter controls (phishing-resistant MFA, compliant devices)
