Allowing OneDrive sync from unmanaged devices copies all synced files to devices without organizational security controls. Personal devices sync corporate files locally without BitLocker, AV, or endpoint security enforcement.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: SharePoint admin center > Settings > Sync > Allow syncing only on computers joined to specific domains
