SharePoint and OneDrive store documents, collaboration artifacts, and file-based records. Without a retention policy, files can be deleted before their required retention period expires, eliminating records needed for compliance, litigation, and forensic investigations.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Microsoft Purview > Data lifecycle management > Retention policies > Create or edit a retention policy to include SharePoint sites and OneDrive accounts
