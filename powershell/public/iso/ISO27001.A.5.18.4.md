Access reviews for privileged roles detect and remove stale, unnecessary, or unauthorized role assignments. Without periodic reviews, accumulation of privileged role assignments creates lateral movement opportunities and violates least-privilege principles.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Entra admin center > Identity Governance > Access reviews > New access review > Review type: Members of a group or Users assigned to a privileged role
