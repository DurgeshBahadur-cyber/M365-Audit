Channel email addresses allow external parties to post messages to Teams channels by sending email to a generated address. This bypasses normal message review and creates an inbound channel that is difficult to monitor for malicious attachments or phishing content.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Teams admin center > Teams settings > Email integration > Users can send emails to a channel email address > Off
