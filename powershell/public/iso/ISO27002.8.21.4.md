Microsoft Secure Score measures the organization's security posture against a set of recommended controls. Without actively tracking and improving it, remediation of security gaps is not systematically prioritized.

#### Remediation action:

Review and act on improvement actions. Microsoft Secure Score > . Assign owners to high-impact actions and track progress

Configure via: security.microsoft.com/securescore > Improvement actions > sort by Score impact
