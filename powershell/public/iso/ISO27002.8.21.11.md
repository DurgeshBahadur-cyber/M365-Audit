Direct send allows unauthenticated email delivery from internal IP ranges without credentials. Without controls on which systems are authorized to send, any device that can reach Exchange Online can send email appearing to originate from your domain — a spoofing and BEC enabler.

#### Remediation action:

Connect to Exchange Online PowerShell to check inbound connector configuration
