LinkedIn account connections share Entra profile data with LinkedIn, a third-party platform outside organizational control. Disabling this prevents accidental data residency violations and reduces the profile data available to social engineering actors.

#### Remediation action:

Prevents data leakage between LinkedIn and organizational directory

Configure via: Entra admin center > Users > User settings > LinkedIn account connections > No
