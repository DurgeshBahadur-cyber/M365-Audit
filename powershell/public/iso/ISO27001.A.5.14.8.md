DKIM signs outbound messages with a domain-specific cryptographic key, allowing receiving servers to verify that mail was not tampered with in transit and genuinely originated from the domain. Without DKIM, spoofed messages claiming to be from the domain will pass unauthenticated and may deceive recipients.

#### Remediation action:

Connect to Exchange Online and verify accepted domains
