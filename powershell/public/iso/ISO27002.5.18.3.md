Access reviews for guest users detect guests who no longer need tenant access. Without reviews, guest accounts accumulate over time and may retain access long after the collaboration project or relationship that required it has ended.

#### Remediation action:

Schedule recurring reviews

Configure via: Entra admin center > Identity Governance > Access reviews > New access review > Review type: Guest users only
