Shareable links that grant access to everyone in the organization allow Power BI content to be accessed by any tenant user who receives the link — regardless of whether they should have access.

#### Remediation action:

Restrict shareable links in Power BI

Configure via: Power BI Admin portal > Tenant settings > Export and sharing settings > Allow shareable links to grant access to everyone in your organization: Disabled or Apply to specific security groups
