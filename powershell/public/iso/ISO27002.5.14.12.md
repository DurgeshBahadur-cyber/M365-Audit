Service principal profiles impersonate multiple user identities, complicating audit attribution and allowing access to data belonging to multiple users under a single service principal.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Power BI Admin Portal > Tenant settings > Developer settings > Allow service principals to create and use profiles > Disabled
