Permanently active privileged roles maintain standing admin access with no time limit or justification requirement. JIT access via PIM limits the window of exposure: even if admin credentials are compromised, the attacker can only act during the brief activation window.

#### Remediation action:

Use eligible assignments with time-bound activation

Configure via: Entra admin center > Identity Governance > Privileged Identity Management > Azure AD roles > Global Administrator > Remove permanent active assignments
