Data retention policies ensure that content is preserved for the required period and disposed of afterward. Without retention policies, data subject to legal holds or regulatory requirements can be prematurely deleted or retained indefinitely without governance.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Microsoft Purview > Data lifecycle management > Retention policies > Create a retention policy to preserve or delete content per your requirements
