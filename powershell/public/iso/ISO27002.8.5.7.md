App instance property lock prevents non-owner users from modifying app properties across tenants in multi-tenant apps. Without it, tenant administrators in other tenants can modify the app's configuration.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Entra admin center > Applications > App management policies > configure a default policy to lock sensitive properties on multi-tenant apps
