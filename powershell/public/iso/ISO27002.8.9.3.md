Safe Links rewrites URLs in emails and Office documents and checks them at click time against Microsoft's threat intelligence. Without it, URLs pointing to malicious sites — including late-stage weaponized links that appear safe at delivery — are not blocked when the user clicks.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Security admin center > Safe Links > Create a policy covering all users
