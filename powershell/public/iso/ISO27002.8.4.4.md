Dial-in users authenticate only with a phone number and meeting code, with no organizational identity verification. Allowing them to bypass the lobby grants unverified attendees direct meeting access.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Teams admin center > Meetings > Meeting policies > Global > Dial-in users can bypass the lobby > Off
