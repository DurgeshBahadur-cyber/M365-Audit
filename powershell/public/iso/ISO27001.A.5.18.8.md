Admin accounts with full E3/E5 licenses have access to productivity services (Exchange, Teams, SharePoint) which increase their attack surface — more services to phish, more sessions to steal. A minimal license reduces the services accessible via a compromised admin credential.

#### Remediation action:

Assign admin accounts minimal licenses (Entra ID P2). Do not assign E3/E5 productivity suites

Configure via: M365 admin center > Users > Active users > Licenses
