A CA policy needs at least one valid subject to evaluate against. If all included users or groups have been deleted, the policy applies to an empty set and is effectively disabled. This means controls like MFA, compliant device, or session frequency that the policy was meant to enforce are silently absent.

#### Remediation action:

For each enabled policy, verify the Users > Include section contains at least one valid, resolvable user, group, or role. Policies with empty include targets should be disabled or remediated with a valid fallback include (e.g., 'All users')

Configure via: Entra admin center > Protection > Conditional Access > Policies
