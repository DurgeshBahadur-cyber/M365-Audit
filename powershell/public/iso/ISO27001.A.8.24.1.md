Sensitivity labels on Power BI content propagate classification metadata to exported files. Without label support, data exported from Power BI loses its sensitivity classification and associated DLP and encryption protections.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Power BI Admin Portal > Tenant settings > Information protection > Allow users to apply sensitivity labels for content > Enabled
