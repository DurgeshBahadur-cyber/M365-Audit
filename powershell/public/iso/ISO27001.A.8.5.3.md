Email OTP authentication sends a one-time code to an email address, which may itself not be MFA-protected. If the email account is compromised, the email OTP provides no additional security — it's a second factor that is only as strong as the first.

#### Remediation action:

Email OTP is a weaker authentication factor

Configure via: Entra admin center > Protection > Authentication methods > Email OTP > Disable
