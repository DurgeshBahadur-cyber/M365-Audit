Publish-to-web creates publicly accessible embed codes for Power BI reports, providing unauthenticated access to the embedded content for anyone with the URL.

#### Remediation action:

Configure via: Power BI Admin Portal > Tenant settings > Export and sharing > Publish to web > Disabled
